Cardiovascular Equipment

There are many options to choose from, including treadmills, stationary bikes, ellipticals, and rowing machines. Each offers different benefits, from building lower body strength to providing low-impact cardio. Pick the machine you enjoy using the most to help you stay motivated.

Strength Training Equipment

Anything that provides resistance, from weights to elastic bands, can be an effective tool for muscle building, strength, and endurance training. With a set of bands in varying weights, you can mimic weighted exercises, including squats, push-ups, planks, and rows.
